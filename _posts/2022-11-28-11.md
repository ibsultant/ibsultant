```python
x = np.arange(0, 6, 0.1)
y1 = np.sin(x)
y2 = np.cos(x)

plt.plot(x, y1, label = 'sin')
plt.plot(x, y2, linestyle = '--', label = 'cos')

plt.xlabel('x')
plt.ylabel('y')
plt.title('sin & cos')
plt.legend() # 범례표시
plt.show()
```
